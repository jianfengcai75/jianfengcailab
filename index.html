Welcome

Welcome to the Jianfeng Cai Research Group! Our team explores cutting-edge studies in chemistry, biophysics, and molecular engineering. Browse our research projects, publications, and group activities through the navigation above.

About Our Work

We are dedicated to advancing chemical and biomolecular research, focusing on innovative molecular design, peptide-based materials, and biomedical applications. Our lab emphasizes interdisciplinary approaches to solve complex scientific challenges.

Research Highlights

  • Peptide Design: Developing functional peptides for biomedical and materials applications.
  • Protein Interactions: Studying molecular mechanisms of signaling pathways and protein-protein interactions.
  • Biophysical Characterization: Applying advanced techniques to understand structure-function relationships.

Each project in our group combines experimental and computational approaches, aiming for impactful real-world applications.
